3. How We Use Data

We use merchant and store data to provide OPS Intelligence features, including store connection, operations analysis, PDF-ready reports, revenue and inventory summaries, churn risk views, pricing opportunities, forecasts, and support.

4. Third-Party Services

OPS Intelligence uses infrastructure and service providers such as hosting, database, payment, email, and AI providers. These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service.

5. Shopify Privacy Webhooks

OPS Intelligence supports Shopify's required privacy webhooks for customer data requests, customer redaction, and shop redaction. When Shopify sends a verified redaction webhook, related stored Shopify connection data is removed or redacted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, export, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. Merchants can also uninstall the app to revoke Shopify access.

7. Contact

Privacy requests can be sent to info@opsintelligence.org.
