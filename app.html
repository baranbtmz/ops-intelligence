Expected columns
OrdersOrder ID, order date, total, status, buyer email.
ProductsItem name, SKU, quantity, price, inventory if available.

Upload Your Own File
Use any CSV, XLS, or XLSX export with commerce columns. OPS automatically detects common names like date, total, status, product, SKU, quantity and customer email.
Column guide
RequiredTotal / amount / revenue column.
RecommendedOrder date, order id, status, quantity, product title, SKU, customer email.
OptionalInventory, price, category. Missing values are estimated conservatively for analysis.
