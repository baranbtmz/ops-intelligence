Why OPS Intelligence?

E-commerce stores generate thousands of data points every day: orders, returns, inventory movements, ad spend. But the vast majority of this data is lost without ever being analyzed. Store owners rely on intuition, ad budgets are wasted, inventory shortages lose customers.

OPS Intelligence was designed to solve this problem. It analyzes your Shopify and Meta Ads data in real time, identifies operational bottlenecks, uncovers root causes and provides a prioritized action plan. Not guesswork — data. Not intuition — analysis.

The Power of Industrial Engineering

OPS Intelligence is managed by a team with an industrial engineering background. This is no coincidence — industrial engineering is one of the world's most powerful disciplines for optimizing complex systems, increasing process efficiency and making data-driven decisions.

We applied the same engineering mindset that enabled Toyota's manufacturing revolution, Amazon's logistics optimization and hospital operational efficiency to e-commerce. The result: a platform that shows where every euro goes and makes every decision data-driven.
