Shopify App Review Demo

OPS Intelligence walkthrough

This demo shows how a merchant signs in, connects commerce data, reviews operational health, and uses OPS Intelligence to act on inventory, revenue, ads, churn, and margin signals.

Step 1

Sign in to the OPS workspace

Merchants access OPS Intelligence through a secure account. Shopify reviewers can use the provided test credentials to open the same workspace experience.

Account-based access for review and merchant use Connector-ready onboarding for store data
Step 2

Review store health in one view

The dashboard summarizes operational health, revenue, fulfillment time, average order value, and cancellation risk so merchants can quickly understand what needs attention.

Health score and key operating metrics Source-labeled reporting for connected data
Step 3

Find ad and inventory conflicts

OPS highlights campaign performance and cross alarms, helping merchants notice when ad spend, stock pressure, or low ROAS could be creating hidden operating losses.

ROAS comparison across campaigns Action suggestions for underperforming segments
Step 4

Turn analysis into pricing actions

Pricing views show product-level margin context and suggested actions, giving founders a practical way to review pricing and bundling opportunities.

Margin recommendations by product Plan-gated advanced analysis for paid tiers
Step 5

Keep billing and access aligned

Shopify billing approvals synchronize with OPS access so merchants who subscribe through Shopify see the right plan inside OPS without a duplicate checkout.

Shopify subscription status reflected in OPS Fallback web billing remains separate for non-Shopify users
